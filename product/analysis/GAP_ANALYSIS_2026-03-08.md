# Zoho CRM Gap Analysis — 2026-03-08
## Zazmic CRM vs. Zoho CRM — Full Feature Comparison

---

## Executive Summary

Zazmic CRM covers the core sales pipeline loop (lead capture → qualification → deal close) with solid CRUD, pipeline management, and a clean UI. Estimated feature parity with Zoho CRM is **~40–45%** of a standard Zoho Professional/Enterprise deployment. The biggest gaps are in automation, email, reporting, custom configuration, and AI-assisted selling.

## What's Working Well

- Core CRUD loop is solid across all 4 modules
- Lead conversion workflow (atomic, no orphaned records)
- Pipeline management with ordered stages and probabilities
- Deal line items and multiple contacts per deal are ahead of many basic CRMs
- Stage change audit trail
- Google OAuth SSO
- Clean, consistent UI with unified ListTable component

## Biggest Gaps by Business Impact

1. **No email integration** — reps still switch to Gmail; no activity is auto-logged
2. **No reporting beyond the dashboard** — management can't run custom reports or forecasts
3. **No lead scoring** — reps have no signal on which leads to prioritize
4. **No task management** — no way to track follow-ups or reminders in the CRM
5. **No bulk import** — onboarding new data requires manual entry or direct DB seeding
6. **No automation** — every action is manual; no notifications, no auto-assign
